Installation Guide

1

Download AIDAA

Click the download button above to get the latest version of AIDAA for your operating system. The download will start automatically.

2

Run the Installer

Windows: Double-click AIDAA-Setup.exe and follow the installation wizard.
macOS: Open the .dmg file and drag AIDAA to Applications.

3

Launch AIDAA

Find AIDAA in your applications menu or desktop and click to launch. The first startup may take a few moments to initialize.

4

Complete Setup

Follow the welcome wizard to configure your preferences, set up voice commands, and customize AIDAA's appearance and behavior.

5

Start Using AIDAA

You're all set! Start chatting with AIDAA, explore the features, and begin automating your daily tasks with your new AI assistant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AIDAA completely free to use?

Yes! AIDAA offers a robust free version with core AI functionality, task reminders, and local data processing. You can upgrade to Pro or Enterprise for advanced features like voice commands, automation, and priority support.

How is my privacy protected?

Privacy is our top priority. AIDAA processes all data locally on your device - your conversations, files, and personal information never leave your computer. We don't collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers.

What languages does AIDAA support?

AIDAA currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, and Korean. We're continuously adding support for more languages based on user demand.

Can I use AIDAA offline?

Yes! Once installed, AIDAA works completely offline. You only need an internet connection for the initial setup and occasional updates. All AI processing happens locally on your device.

How often is AIDAA updated?

We release updates monthly with new features, improvements, and bug fixes. Major updates with significant new capabilities are released quarterly. All updates are free for existing users.

Can I integrate AIDAA with other applications?

Yes! AIDAA Pro and Enterprise plans include integration capabilities with popular applications like Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, Slack, Trello, and many more. The Enterprise plan also provides API access for custom integrations.
